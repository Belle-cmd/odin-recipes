Creamy Hot Chocolate

Perfect for milk lovers and chocoholics! This is a must-have-drink! Keep some condensed milk on hand for cold days, and whip up a batch of this rich hot chocolate warm your loved ones.

- Crafted by Eagle brand

  • Prep: barely 5 mins
  • Cook: 10 mins
  • Total: barely 15 mins
  • Servings: 8
  • Yield: 2 quarts

Steps

If using a stove...

  1. In large saucepan over medium heat, combine sweetened condensed milk, cocoa, vanilla and salt; mix well.
  2. lowly stir in water. Heat through, stirring occasionally. Do not boil.
  3. Top with marshmallows, (optional)
  4. Store covered in refrigerator.

If using a microwave...

  1. In 2-quart glass measure, combine all ingredients except marshmallows.
  2. Microwave on 100% power (HIGH) 8 to 10 minutes, stirring every 3 minutes.
  3. Top with marshmallows if desired. Store covered in refrigerator.

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 ounce) can EAGLE BRAND® Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa
  • 1½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 6½ cups hot water
  • 3 10 miniatures Mini marshmallows

Cook's Notes

  • Creamy Hot Chocolate can be stored in refrigerator up to 5 days.
  • Mix well and reheat before serving.
